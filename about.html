Who We Are

At Tongue Feast (Siddhivinayak Products), we believe spices are more than ingredients – they are the soul of flavor. Rooted in tradition and crafted with care, our blends bring the authentic taste of India to kitchens around the world.

Manufactured at MIDC Chikalthana, Chh. Sambhaji Nagar, Maharashtra — we source the finest raw materials, hand-roast them using traditional techniques, and grind them to perfection. No artificial colors. No preservatives. No shortcuts.

Leadership

The Visionary Behind Our Brand

Mr. Rahul Gondkar

Founder & Director, Siddhivinayak Products

Mr. Rahul Gondkar is a visionary entrepreneur and the driving force behind Siddhivinayak Products. With 9 years of industry experience and roots closely connected to agriculture, he understands the true value of quality raw materials and the hard work behind them.

Since establishing the company in 2016, he has combined traditional values with modern manufacturing practices to build a brand focused on purity, consistency, and trust.

His leadership reflects a strong commitment not only to business growth but also to supporting farmers and delivering authentic, high-quality spices to every customer.
